How do I claim a student discount for Apple products in India?

Ans: Conditions to follow-
  • A current college ID card
  • One should be at most 18 years old.
  • The discount is only available to students in grades 12 and up.
You can use the discount through an Apple premium reseller or online store.

What proof do I need to get an Apple Store education discount?

Ans: Valuation proof of eligibility is required to get an Apple Store education discount. For this purpose, you typically need to provide proof of your academic status, such as a valid student ID or acceptance letter from a university.

Can I get a student discount at the Unicorn Store?

Ans: Yes, student discounts are usually offered to those who can provide valid proof of their status as a student, such as a student ID or a university email address. It is best to check with the Unicorn Store for their specific requirements and eligibility criteria.

How good are Unicorn's Apple student discount offers?

Ans: Apple student discount offers are typically quite good and can save students money on new Apple products. The discount typically ranges from 10 -15% and may include additional perks. Students can take advantage of these discounts by providing proof of enrollment in an eligible university.

Does Apple give a discount on iPad to students?

Ans: Yes, Apple Student Discount is a fantastic way to save money on the tech giant's most popular products and services. The deal typically ranges from 20% to 50% off the regular price of an iPad, depending on the model.

Does Apple launch a back-to-school offer every year for India?

Ans: Yes, Apple has previously offered back-to-school deals in various countries, including India, offering discounts on Apple products to students and educators during the back-to-school season. It is best to check Apple's website or social media channels for any current promotions or offers.
